Lorna Fox - Sivananda Teacher

Lorna has been practising yoga for over 10 years, following her daily Sivananda practice of asanas, pranayama and meditation. Lorna first discovered Sivananda Hatha yoga on a trip to southern India and a visit to the Sivananda yoga ashram in Tamil Nadu. She was deeply inspired by the teachings, including the asanas and pranayama, and also the philosophy known as Vedanta. Lorna continued to practise yoga and follow the five principles through her life, deciding to dedicate herself to her practice more strongly and to carry out her yoga teacher training in 2018 at the International Sivananda Yoga Vedanta Centre in Netala, Uttarkashi in the Himalayas. Lorna passed her 200 hours TTC to become a Yoga Siromani by the name of Devaki - hence the name, Devaki Yoga. Lorna has now been teaching for over 3 years. Lorna's practice, and teaching is deep and transformational. She guides her students towards the essence of yoga, to find the true meaning of yoga - joining - to join the body, mind and spirit and create deeper peace through daily life as well as greater health, fitness and vitality. Lorna teaches weekly classes both online and in person, as well as teaching corporate classes.

Jane Doe - Sivananda Teacher

Jane has been practising yoga for over 10 years and qualified at the International Yoga Vedanta Centre in Netala, Uttarkashi in the Himalayas in 2018. Jane passed her 200 hours TTC to become a Yoga Siromani and has been teaching weekly classes for over 3 years. Jane practises both Hatha and Ashtanga Vinyasa yoga and brings this dynamism into her daily practise and her teachings. Jane is a dynamic yoga teacher and brings her students on a journey to greater strength and flexibility through her classes, in turn, improving their focus, health and stamina. Jane teaches weekly classes both online and in person, as well as teaching corporate classes.
